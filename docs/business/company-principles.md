# Company Principles

## Purpose

Build technology that solves real business problems.

---

## Principle 1

Customer success comes before technology.

We build solutions because they solve a business problem—not because they use the latest tools.

---

## Principle 2

Security by Design.

Every solution should consider security from the beginning.

---

## Principle 3

Automation First.

If a repetitive task can be automated safely, we automate it.

---

## Principle 4

Everything is Documented.

Documentation is part of the product, not an afterthought.

---

## Principle 5

Keep It Simple.

Simple systems are easier to maintain, secure, and scale.

---

## Principle 6

Continuous Improvement.

Every project should leave us better than before.

---

## Principle 7

Cloud Native.

Prefer scalable cloud-native solutions where appropriate.

---

## Principle 8

Transparency.

Be honest with clients about costs, risks, and timelines.

---

## Principle 9

Quality Over Quantity.

Deliver fewer things exceptionally well instead of many things poorly.

---

## Principle 10

Learn Every Day.

Technology changes constantly.
Our company commits to continuous learning.
